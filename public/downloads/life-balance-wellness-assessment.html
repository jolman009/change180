How to Take This Assessment

This assessment covers six key areas of life. For each statement, rate how true it feels for you right now — not where you want to be, but where you honestly are today.

Instructions:

  1. Read each statement carefully
  2. Circle the number (1-5) that best represents your current reality
  3. Be honest — this is for your eyes only
  4. Add up your score for each section
  5. Transfer your section totals to the results page
  6. Reflect on what your scores reveal about your life right now
